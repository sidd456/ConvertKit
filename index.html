Quitting Wall Street to become a creator

Khe Hy had everything he’d dreamed of as a self-described “nerdy” kid from an immigrant family - a high-paying job in Wall Street, a fancy New York City apartment, and as many shoes as he wanted, the kind he could never afford as a kid. But he wasn’t happy. No matter how much money he made, promotions he got, or expensive things he could buy, he just didn’t feel alive. ​ But there was something he was doing on the side that did light him up. So he quit his job on Wall Street in hopes to find a way to make a living doing something he loved.
